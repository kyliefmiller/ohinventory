Welcome to the Southwest Oral Histories Inventory!

Through Southwest Colorado's 250-150 initiative, this inventory was created to compile all of the oral histories that tell stories about the region. This region, as defined through the 250-150 initiative, spans the counties of Archuleta, Dolores, Hinsdale, La Plata, Montezuma, Ouray, San Juan, and San Miguel in Southwest Colorado.

Through this website, one can search the inventory to find oral histories and learn more about the region. We hope this resource can be helpful to researchers and anybody else interested in learning through oral histories.
